About Us

We believe that life gets a lot easier when you know what's around you.

Citywiz is a free and easy to use Iphone app (soon on android) that will help you discover, find and connect to everything local at any city of Puerto Rico (soon other countries).

Whether you are a visitor or a local, looking for a place to satisfy your cravings , have a nice dinner, going for a night out ,maybe looking to get the local intel on events and activities around you or even searching for a new house to buy or rent, our app will help you find and stay connected to everything your city has to offer.

New city wizards (guides) will be added regularly for you to discover cool and necessary things like food, music, art, events, sports, living, places to stay, professional services, touring and shopping.
